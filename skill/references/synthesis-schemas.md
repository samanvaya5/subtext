# Synthesis Schemas

Schemas for Stage 5 (Cross-talk Synthesis) and Stage 6 (Application). These are used after all per-talk analyses are complete.

---

## Cross-Talk Synthesis

This is the primary synthesis artifact. It finds patterns across ALL individual analyses and produces a "strategic intelligence memo."

### Schema

```markdown
# Cross-Talk Synthesis: [Research Topic/Source]

**Date:** [Date of synthesis]
**Corpus:** [N] videos analyzed from [source/channel/topic]
**Time Range:** [Date range of content]

---

## Consensus Themes

### Tier 1: Near-Universal (80%+ of talks touch on this)
- [Theme]: [Which talks, what they say]
- ...

### Tier 2: Strong Signal (50-80% of talks)
- [Theme]: [Which talks, what they say]
- ...

### Tier 3: Emerging (20-50% — watch this space)
- [Theme]: [Which talks, what they say]
- ...

## Contrarian Positions & Active Debates
- [Debate topic]: [Side A (who)] vs. [Side B (who)] — [who's more convincing and why]
- ...

## Recurring Pain Points (Ranked by Severity)
1. [Pain point] — Severity: Critical | Mentioned by: [N] talks
2. ...
3. ...

## Emerging Architecture Patterns
- [Pattern name]: [Description] — [Who's using it] — [Maturity: experimental/adopted/mature]
- ...

## What's NOT Being Discussed (Blind Spots)
- [Gap 1]: [Why this matters and what it implies about the industry's blind spots]
- ...

## Power Dynamics & Hidden Agendas
- [Observation about who's positioning themselves how, who's selling what, strategic partnerships hinted at]
- ...

## Signal Strength Assessment

| Signal | Strength | Source Talks | Actionability |
|--------|----------|-------------|---------------|
| [Signal 1] | Strong/Moderate/Weak | [Talk names] | High/Medium/Low |
| ... | ... | ... | ... |

---
*Synthesized with Subtext*
```

## Opportunity Map

An optional artifact for Stage 6, focused on actionable predictions and startup/product opportunities.

### Schema

```markdown
# Opportunity Map: [Research Topic]

**Based on:** [N] video analyses from [source]
**Timeframe:** Predictions for 6-12 months from [date]

---

## Where the Puck Is Going: Falsifiable Predictions

1. [Prediction] — Falsifiable by: [what would prove this wrong] — Confidence: [High/Medium/Low]
2. ...

## The Opportunity Map: Top Opportunities

| # | Opportunity | Category | Why Now | Market Size | Competition | Build Difficulty | Verdict |
|---|------------|----------|---------|-------------|-------------|-----------------|---------|
| 1 | [Name] | [Category] | [Trigger] | [Estimate] | [Who else] | [Easy/Medium/Hard] | [Strong Buy/Buy/Watch/Skip] |
| ... |

## What to IGNORE (Anti-patterns & Commoditized Layers)
- [Anti-pattern 1]: [Why it's a trap]
- ...

## The Ideal Build Stack (If building in this space today)
[Describe the ideal tech stack based on what the corpus reveals about what works]

## Risk Assessment
- [Risk 1]: [Likelihood] — [Impact] — [Mitigation]
- ...

---
*Mapped with Subtext*
```

## Idea Evaluation

An optional artifact for Stage 6, where the user's own idea/product is evaluated against the corpus.

### Schema

```markdown
# Idea Evaluation: [Idea Name]

**Date:** [Date]
**Idea Source:** [User's document/description]
**Evaluated Against:** [N] video analyses from [source]

---

## Idea Summary
[Brief restatement of the user's idea in your own words — confirms you understood it]

## Viability Assessment
### What Aligns (Corpus supports this)
- [Evidence point 1]
- ...

### What Contradicts (Corpus challenges this)
- [Challenge 1]
- ...

### What's Missing (Idea doesn't address)
- [Gap 1]
- ...

## Competitive Landscape
- [Who's building something similar based on corpus signals]
- ...

## Enhancement Recommendations
- [Recommendation 1]: [Why, based on corpus evidence]
- ...

## Verdict: Lead or Chase?
[Is this idea ahead of the market (leading) or following established trends (chasing)? What would it take to lead?]

---
*Evaluated with Subtext*
```
